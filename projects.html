James Webb Space Telescope Reserach Project

My first project was a research project for my APCSP final. We were assigned to research about a tech innovation in the past few years. I chose the James Webb Space Telescope becuase it has interested me and I have been following its progress for a while.

This project included research about data, creating a infographic, and presenting a slideshow. I had fun making the infographic because I like graphic design.

I learned more about the telescope through the lense of data. This research was meaning full becuase it increased my knowledge and interest in space.



Dragonfly Zoom Zoom Zoom Art

This project was part of my freshman year art class. Our project was to create a "zoom zoom zoom" drawing (where some parts of the image were zoomed in) out of colored pencils.

This project entailed finding a reference picture, scale some parts of the image to create the "zoom zoom zoom" effect, sketch the outline, and add color with colored pencils.

I really enjoyed this project becuase I love working with colored pencils. I learned a lot about blending and layering colored pencils. This was my favorite project of the year and I am really happy with how it turned out.
